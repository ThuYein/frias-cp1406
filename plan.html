1. The target audience for the site would be kids under the age of 13 and their parents.
2. In order to appeal to this target audience the site would be designed with large attractive images and colorful elements

Content

  • Home Page : About this organization
  • Events : Information about the events such as promotion and discounts
  • Products : Information about the products available online
  • Order form : Allows customers to order products
  • Services : the services we provide online
  • FAQ : Frequently Asked Questions about the services
  • Feedback Form : Allows the customer to give feedback to us
  • Cafe : Information about the upcoming new cafe
